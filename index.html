03 About

I'm a Senior Content Writer, Copywriter, and Social Media Specialist based in Istanbul, with over a decade of experience writing in both Arabic and English across healthcare, real estate, education, hospitality, and humanitarian sectors.

My background started in journalism at Qatar Tribune, which is still how I approach content now: verify the claim, source the quote, structure for clarity, then make it readable. That habit carries through everything from medical SEO articles to press releases to short-form video scripts.

I hold a Master of Arts from Hamad Bin Khalifa University, where my thesis examined the education and career choices of Qatari women in the media sector, research that sharpened the same instinct for structured argument and evidence I bring to long-form strategy work today.

I work comfortably across both languages independently, not translating one into the other, but writing each the way a native reader expects it.
